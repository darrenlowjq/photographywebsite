ABOUT ME

I am a scuba diver and underwater photographer living in Singapore. I prefer to shoot macro critters underwater, though I occasionally shoot larger marine creatures or landscapes too.

I started scuba diving in 2013 while I was an undergraduate at Singapore Management University, eventually taking my first step as a dive professional by qualifying as a PADI Divemaster in 2015.

Having fallen in love with the ocean and all the wonders it has to offer, I dabble in underwater photography to share the beauty of the underwater world with other divers and non-divers alike.

All photos on this site were shot with with my trusty Sony RX100 in a Nauticam housing.
